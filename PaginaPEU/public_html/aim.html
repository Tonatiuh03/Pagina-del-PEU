University Space Program Mission

  • Integrate, coordinate and enhance space science and technology national community.

  • Support the creation of infrastructure to develop space science and technology at UNAM

  • Promote and support multidisciplinary education programs.

  • Perform strategic studies in space science and technology to prospect its growth in Mexico, and support the development of the University Space Plan and Mexico’s National Plan of Space Activities.

  • Take part in relevant critical decisions and provide advice in public policies development.
